Tour 1

Day 1 Board your scheduled flight to arrive at Male International airport. After a safe arrival at Male, our tour representative will guide and transfer you to Meeru Island Resort via Speedboat. After your check-in, use the rest of the day to explore the resort and its beautiful surroundings. Relax and stay overnight at the resort.

Day 2 Enjoy your breakfast at the resort and spend the day at the spectacular beaches of Maldives. Go water skiing, windsurfing, snorkelling, or play beach basketball and volleyball with your group. Alternatively, you may explore Male on foot. Check out the markets and famous landmarks and shop for some memorabilia to take home. Head back to the resort for a delicious dinner. Overnight stay at the resort.
